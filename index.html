    • Hi!

      My name is Caleb. I am currently a senior design student at Carnegie Mellon University and previously a brand design intern at Dropbox.

      I'm a firm believer that designed brands create moments of joy and connection that enrich existing products. But as time goes on and companies grow, brands can become limited or unimaginative, and lose consistency. My work involves nurturing brands through change without losing the energy and joy that they inspire. While I consider myself a generalist within my practice, I'm super excited about interactive, moving, and strategic design systems that inspire, whether on digital or traditional surfaces.
