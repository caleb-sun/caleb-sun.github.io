  • CSL Behring is a bio-pharmaceutical company that develops therapies in various areas. It manages CSL Plasma, one of the world's largest plasma collection networks.

  • • Overview

    Team organization and responsibility breakdown

  • I interned at CSL Behring in the summer of 2023 and continued on contract through fall of 2023. During that period, I was a part of an internal SCRUM team working on the Plasma Digital Product ecosystem. The SCRUM Team was made up of around 12 members spanning the development, management, QA, and design disciplines, and worked within a 2-week sprint structure.

    Beyond my work with the SCRUM team, I played a crucial role in two longer-term projects which are covered in depth below. These were collaborative efforts primarily within the designs on the team.

  • • Redesign

    Improving and humanizing the Plasma Donor app

  • A series of short 2-week sprint projects redesigning key screens in the Plasma Donor Experiences. Each redesign was aimed at improving the most used features of the app with as little overhaul of existing designs as possible, given the limited dev resources.

  • • EU Documentation

    Creating crucial documentation to prepare for a new app launch

  • Two extensive projects completed over a month to document all screens and interactions of the EU-facing Plasma donor app.

  • • Workflow Redesign

    Re-evaluating the way CSL designs.

  • This project was a holistic design-Ops endeavor to re-evaluate and provide solutions to the inefficiencies in our design team, including how we were leveraging our design and project management tools and communicating with one another.
