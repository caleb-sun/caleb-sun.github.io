    Concluding Thoughts

    These series of projects really challenged me. Having such a prolific designer and expansive body of work to contend with, I struggled a lot with generating a direction for these artifacts creatively. What was the turning point was me realizing that an expereince can be all that you need to define a 'brand'. What made my projects fit together was the experience of discovering each and every projects, and capturing that sensation that drew me to Guang Yu in the first place was the defining factor of the design system.
