    Vela Projects is a construction consulting firm offering Project Management and Owner's Representative Services based in Portland, Oregon. Their mission is to help clients successfully navigate the complexities of building projects.

    Branding

    I was initially brought on to refine and refresh the existing branding. Vela Projects already had a logo with a personal story to the partners, so I took a conservative approach to refine the logo geometrically, and identify a cohesive color palette that reflected the sincere and earnest nature of the company.

    Materials

    The next phase of the project was the create branded materials for the client's needs. I worked with the client to identify the required documents for future success, and outlined a plan for how to adapt the new branding package to a google-based platform. I took into careful consideration how each element would be scalable and accessible to the client after handoff given their existing Google-based platform.

    Website

    The final phase of this project is to design a website based on the client's needs, including setting up the site for further managable maintainance. For this reason, we used Wix. The website was first mocked up on Figma, and then transfered onto Wix with dynamic content.
