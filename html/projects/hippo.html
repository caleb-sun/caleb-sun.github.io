About

    Redesigning a website for a local swim school.

    Skills

    Web Design
    Design Consulting

    Duration

    5 weeks, Summer 2023

    Tools

    Figma
    Wix

    Hippo Swim is a small swim school based in the Bay Area that had grown beyond what their initial platform could support. As they decided to expand business-wise, they needed a website that serves as an effective resource and landing page for their customers and students.

    Branding

    Hippo Swim already has a logo incorporated on-site, so I refrained from examining the logo too heavily. Instead, I focused on creating an effective and compatible branding package sufficient for web-design purposes. I also created illustrations inspired from the logo to create more charm and child-like play in the site.
