    Five weeks
    Summer 2023

    Tools

    Figma
    Webflow

    Hippo Swim is a small swim school based in the Bay Area that had grown beyond what their initial platform could support. As they decided to expand business-wise, they needed a website that serves as an effective resource and landing page for their customers and students.

    Branding

    Hippo Swim already had a logo incorporated on-site, so I refrained from reworking anything already set in stone. Instead, I focused on creating an effective and compatible branding package sufficient for web-design purposes. I also created illustrations inspired from the logo to create more charm and child-like play.

    Website Design

    After establishing a base design system for the website, I created mockups of major pages for the site, allowing the client to make design tweeks before the development process. This was also an opportunity for me to showcase the different breakpoints of the site.
