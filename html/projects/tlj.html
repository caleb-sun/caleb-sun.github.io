    Reimagining the customer and associate experience at a bakery.

    Skills

    Duration

    2 weeks, Spring 2023

    Tools

    Figma

    Tous les Jours is an international bakery and cafe chain based in South Korea. They specialize in French-inspired pastries and Asian-style cakes, and are a cornerstone in many communities throughout America.

    Overview

    I worked at my local Tous les Jours in high school and created a website for them during my time there, allowing them to take cake orders online. This project revisits that problem space to reimagine the way technology can support the bakery's operations, through both a customer-facing mobile app and an associate-facing tablet app.
