    Project

    Auditing and redesigning the CSL Plasma Donor App as a part of the Plasma SCRUM team.

    Skills

    UI Design
    UX Design
    Information Architecture
    SCRUM Methodologies

    Duration

    12 weeks, Summer 2023

    Tools

    Figma
    Jira

    CSL's reverse-sale plasma donation model is the backbone of all biotherapies and advancements in the medical field. A core element of the plasma donation process is the donor-facing app, assisting donors in getting the most our of their time and energy spent at CSL donation centers. Having recently transitioned from using external vendors for design and development, there was an opportunity for hands-on customization to the donor app, with a priority in improving the user experience in simple and intuitive ways without overburdening developers.

    Poster

    The poster is supposed to be a "first-impression" of Guang Yu, and I decided to represent his work in the way that I had initially found it, with all the intricately crafted artifacts laid out on a sparse website. This method of discovering Guang Yu's work that I developed in the first piece reappears in all the different artifacts.
